PRO Engineering Kit for Fine Gold Recovery

Comprehensive engineering solution to design, calculate and optimize fine gold recovery plants (alluvial gold). Specifically developed for investors, mining companies and engineering managers seeking to maximize extraction efficiency and minimize losses in their operations.

Ideal for:
  • Gold mining investors;
  • Exploration and exploitation companies;
  • Plant engineers;
  • Operations managers;
  • Entrepreneurs planning to launch or improve processing plants;

The Problem

Most gold recovery projects face significant losses due to inadequate designs, poorly sized equipment and lack of specialized technical advice. This results in:

  • Loss of 30-50% of fine gold due to poorly selected equipment;
  • Erroneous investments of $20,000-$100,000 in inadequate equipment;
  • Months of trial and error before achieving stable operation;
  • Absence of reliable technical documentation for implementation;
  • Unreliable advisors causing delays and cost overruns.

The Solution

The PRO Engineering Kit is a complete solution that includes everything necessary to design, calculate and start up a fine gold recovery plant with guaranteed technical efficiency.

  • Eliminates costly errors in equipment selection and sizing;
  • Maximizes gold recovery up to 85-95% vs 35-55% without advice;
  • Reduces implementation time from 6-12 months to just 2-3 months;
  • Provides certified technical documentation for financing and investment;
  • Guarantees project profitability through accurate ROI calculations.

Our service combines professional technical analysis, certified engineering calculations, 3D equipment models and continuous support during the implementation phase.
